Ostroske City Rating Scale - 12 Points

The cost of living in Mega City 12 is "Moderate", which adds 2 points on the OCRS. You can rent a small apartment for approximately $3,500 cyber credits a month.

The crime rating in the city is "Very High". This adds 0 points on the OCRS. The good news is that our security forces are very active!

The availability of entertainment is "Decent". This adds 2 points on the OCRS. It is reasonably easy, and safe, to attend a show or a concert!

The pollution in the city is "Heavy/Smog". This adds 0 points to the OCRS. This allows our sanitation and waste departments to function at a high capacity, saving the taxpayers money!

The general scenery and architectural layout is "Modern Drab". This adds 1 point on the OCRS. No wasted money on maintaining a luxury look for MC12!

The taxes in Mega City 12 go towards funding our security and sanitation services. They are rated as "Above Average", which adds 1 point on the Ostroske City Rating Scale.

You will find easy transporation and walking to any of the city's hospital or detention centers. This adds 3 points to the OCRS.

Situated at the base of an active volcano, Mega City 12 is always "warm". This adds 3 points to the OCRS, but your heating bills have never been lower!
