The Twelve Rules of Mega City Twelve Are As Follows

  1. Do not, under any circumstances, look a robot in the eyes
  2. Humans are not allowed out of their dwellings after dark
  3. If you visit the volcano, you must take one piece of litter back with you
  4. You can never question the supreme overlord, Mount Vulcan
  5. Please be kind and rewind
  6. The only acceptable ice cream toppings are nuts and bolts, motor oil, and sprinkles
  7. If you move to the city, you can never leave, for any reason, ever
  8. Do not feed the robots after midnight
  9. All innovation must be properly planned and accounted for
  10. Please schedule your sick time 365 days in advance
  11. Babies must learn to talk by the age of 2 or they will visit Mount Vulcan
  12. Please follow all rules and regulations, and remember, fun is mandatory!
