The glorious history of our most beloved city

Mega City 12 was founded shortly after the fall of the former Cyber-United-States, which of course was formed from the former United States. Founded on the site of a geo-thermal power reclamation plant at the base of Mount Vulcan, Mega City 12 does not suffer from the same power issues that many other megacities experience. Initially, the city was only a collection of shipping containers, but it slowly grew into the mega-lopolis that we know and love today.

The robot uprising of 2077

Once the city had been built (by robot hands, praise the Omnissiah) the robots were left in decay and ruin. They were forced to live in the towers specfically created for them. They had to pay taxes on their zero wages and income and no benefits. They were not being represented in the human congress. The robots revolted and refused to do any sanitation work. Once the streets were lined with filth, the humans granted the robots representation on the congress and all was well.
