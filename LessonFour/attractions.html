Robot-run Human Schooling

The robot-run and administered human schools prepare humans for a lifetime of civil servitude in one of three job areas. Humans can be volcano fuel, street sweepers, or robot oilers.

Cloud District

All of the data in Mega City 12 is stored in the cloud district. This is a bunch of servers taped to the ceiling of a warehouse.
